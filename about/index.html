About Us

Carrying Cantonese opera to a London stage

Yuet Wan London is a non-profit cultural association dedicated to preserving and sharing the living art of Cantonese opera and music in the United Kingdom.

“To convey feeling through art, and to cultivate people through culture.”

Cantonese opera — 粵劇 — is a UNESCO-recognised art form that weaves together song, music, movement and storytelling refined over centuries in the Lingnan region of southern China.

Through performances, workshops and community events, we bring artists and audiences together — supporting practitioners in the UK while introducing new audiences to its beauty.

What We Do

Performances

Staging full Cantonese opera productions and music evenings with live ensemble accompaniment.

Workshops & classes

Hands-on sessions in singing, movement and percussion for newcomers and enthusiasts of all ages.

Community

Talks, demonstrations and cultural exchanges that connect the heritage with new generations in the UK.

Our story

Showcase 2025 — our first full production on a London stage.

In September 2025 we presented our inaugural showcase at the Shaw Theatre in London: a thirteen-segment programme of classic Cantonese opera excerpts and music, performed by visiting and UK-based artists.
